About:
Try your hand at guessing the correct ability name based on picture of an ability in League of Legends.

Manual:
https://wouter17.github.io/Abilities/Manual/HTML/Abilities.html

Notes:
At first the module may seem daunting with the amount of abilities. However, after a few playthroughs you'll start to get a feel for what kind of name fits with each ability and you may be able to solo this.

Source Code:
